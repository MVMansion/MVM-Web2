Event Venue for Celebrations

Host your wedding, celebration, rehearsal, or special event meal at Mountain View Mansion. Now available for groups of 50 or fewer.

  • Event must be accompanied by 4+ night stay renting entire Mansion
  • Day guests: $10 per person (may not stay past 11:00 PM)
  • Limited chairs, tables, and linens available
  • Parking limited to 15 cars on-site
  • Additional parking at Palisades Park (3 blocks north) - shuttling recommended
  • All events must end by 10:00 PM (noise ordinance)
  • Outdoor music/PA systems permitted with limited volume
