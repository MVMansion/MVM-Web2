Mansion Overview

Utah's Premier 20,000 Square Foot Vacation Estate

Welcome to Mountain View Mansion

Mountain View Mansion is Utah's premier luxury vacation rental, offering an unprecedented 20,000 square feet of elegantly appointed living space on 1.7 private acres. Nestled at the mouth of scenic Provo Canyon with breathtaking views of Mount Timpanogos, our estate provides the perfect setting for unforgettable family reunions, corporate retreats, weddings, and special celebrations.

With accommodations for up to 50 guests across 11 uniquely themed bedroom suites, three fully-equipped commercial kitchens, and world-class amenities including an indoor pool, multiple sport courts, and a professional theater room, Mountain View Mansion delivers an unparalleled hospitality experience.

Year-Round Indoor Pool Complex

Our spectacular indoor pool facility features floor-to-ceiling glass walls that frame panoramic mountain views, creating an immersive connection with the stunning Utah landscape. The heated pool is perfect for all seasons and includes:

  • 20-foot water slide
  • Diving board
  • Large spa/jacuzzi with jets
  • Basketball hoop for pool games
  • Polycarbonate clear roof for natural light
  • Full kitchen and dining area adjacent
  • Men's and women's changing rooms with showers

Spectacular Common Areas

The mansion features multiple gathering spaces designed to accommodate large groups while maintaining an intimate, welcoming atmosphere. The three-story vaulted Great Room serves as the heart of the home with:

  • Soaring wooden beam ceilings reaching three stories high
  • Grand stone fireplace
  • Large screen TV with entertainment system
  • Baby grand piano in formal living room
  • Professional theater room with retractable screen
  • Billiards room with multiple seating areas

Three Professional Kitchens

Designed to serve large groups with ease, our commercial-grade kitchens are located on multiple levels for maximum convenience. The main kitchen features:

  • Sub-Zero refrigerator/freezer
  • 8-burner commercial range with dual ovens
  • Large island with seating for 8
  • Multiple sinks and dishwashers
  • Reverse osmosis water system
  • Formal dining room seating 14+
  • Breakfast nook with mountain views

Indoor & Outdoor Recreation

Stay active with our extensive sport facilities, perfect for all ages and skill levels:

  • Full indoor basketball/volleyball court
  • Outdoor tennis/pickleball/basketball court
  • Regulation shuffleboard
  • Foosball table
  • Pool table that converts to ping pong
  • Yoga mats and ballet bar
  • Large sauna accommodating 15 people

Expansive Outdoor Grounds

The 1.7-acre gated property offers extensive outdoor recreation and relaxation areas:

  • Large playground with rubberized surface
  • In-ground trampoline
  • Multiple swing sets
  • Covered gazebo with seating
  • Multiple deck levels with mountain views
  • Fire pit and outdoor grills
  • Seasonal creek and mature landscaping
