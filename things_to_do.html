üè° Other Features

Mountain View Mansion offers a fully fenced property with multiple outdoor spaces to relax and enjoy. The main deck, located outside the kitchen, is perfect for gatherings, while the Paris Master room has its own private deck. A lower deck overlooks the canal, leading to a cozy patio. The grounds feature fruit trees (seasonal), and for added fun, scooters and bicycles are available for kids. You can also enjoy paddleboarding, or take a short walk to nearby Palisade Park and the mini-golf course.
