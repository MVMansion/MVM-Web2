World-Class Amenities

Everything you need for an unforgettable stay

Indoor Pool Complex

Our stunning year-round heated indoor pool is the crown jewel of Mountain View Mansion. Surrounded by floor-to-ceiling glass walls, the pool area offers breathtaking panoramic mountain views while you swim, creating a truly unique experience in any season.

  • Heated Indoor Pool
  • Water Slide
  • Diving Board
  • Basketball Hoop
  • Large Spa/Jacuzzi
  • Glass Sliding Doors & Clear Roof
  • Pool House Kitchen
  • Changing Rooms
  • Sauna

Indoor Sport Court

Stay active year-round in our indoor sport court. Whether it's a friendly game of basketball, an intense volleyball match, or badminton with the family, our indoor court provides endless entertainment for all ages and skill levels.

  • Basketball Court
  • Volleyball Net
  • Badminton Setup
  • Pickleball Equipment
  • Shuffleboard
  • Foosball Table
  • Yoga Mats
  • Ballet Bar

Outdoor Sport Court

Take your game outdoors on our fenced sport court. With mountain views as your backdrop, enjoy tennis, pickleball, basketball, or volleyball. The court includes a drinking fountain and is perfect for tournaments or casual play.

  • Tennis Court

Theater & Presentation Room

Our professional theater room features a retractable projection screen, blackout curtains, and comfortable seating for movie nights, presentations, or special events. The room includes a pool table and multiple seating areas for versatile entertainment.

  • Retractable Screen
  • 4K Projector
  • Blackout Curtains
  • Surround Sound
  • Billiards Table
  • Multiple Seating Areas
  • Gas Fireplace
  • Roku Streaming
  • Xbox Gaming

Formal Living & Music Room

The elegant formal living room features a baby grand piano, soaring 20-foot ceilings, and luxurious seating arrangements. Perfect for intimate concerts, cocktail hours, or quiet reading time away from the group.

  • Baby Grand Piano
  • Keyboard (Lower Level)
  • 20-Foot Ceilings
  • Multiple Seating Areas
  • Gas Fireplace
  • Large Windows

Three Kitchens

Designed to serve large groups effortlessly, our three fully-equipped kitchens span multiple levels. The main kitchen features top-of-the-line Sub-Zero appliances, multiple cooking stations, and seating for 8 at the island.

  • Sub-Zero Refrigerators
  • 8-Burner Gas Ranges
  • Dual Ovens (Multiple)
  • 4 Dishwashers
  • Multiple Sinks
  • Reverse Osmosis Water
  • 2 Microwaves per Kitchen
  • Ice Maker

Multiple Dining Spaces

With capacity to seat 50+ guests comfortably across multiple dining areas, Mountain View Mansion is perfect for banquets, family dinners, and corporate meals. The formal dining room features a cherry wood table, while additional tables and chairs are available throughout.

  • Formal Dining Room (14+)
  • Breakfast Nook (10)
  • Kitchen Island Seating (8)
  • Pool House Dining
  • Lower Level Dining (30+)
  • Additional Tables Available

Children's Paradise
