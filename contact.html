Frequently Asked Questions

How many guests can you accommodate?

Mountain View Mansion sleeps up to 50 guests comfortably across 11 bedroom suites with 48+ beds and additional pullout options.

What is the minimum stay?

Minimum stay varies by season. Typically 2-3 nights, with longer minimums during peak holiday periods. Contact us for specific dates.

Is the property wheelchair accessible?

Yes! We have an elevator serving the main 3 levels, wheelchair ramps can be setup at entrances, and an accessible bathrooms with grab bars is on the main level.

What amenities are included?

All amenities are included: indoor pool, spa, sport courts, theater, sauna, all kitchens, playground, and outdoor recreation. No hidden fees!

Do you allow pets?

Please contact us to discuss your specific situation. We evaluate pet requests on a case-by-case basis.

Can we host a wedding at the property?

Yes, the mansion can be used as destination wedding venue with stunning mountain backdrops, multiple ceremony locations, and space for up to 50 overnight guests. Minimum stay of 4 nights required

Is catering available?

We can arrange catering services, or you can use our three kitchens to prepare your own meals. Catering is an optional add-on.

What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is at 5:00 PM and check-out is at 10:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request for an additional fee.
