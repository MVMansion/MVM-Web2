Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Important: Please Read Carefully

We understand that plans can change. Please review our cancellation policy before booking. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect your investment.

Cancellation Timeline

60+ Days Before Check-In

Full refund minus $200 processing fee

30-59 Days Before Check-In

50% refund of total booking amount

Less Than 30 Days Before Check-In

No refund. Option to reschedule within 12 months subject to availability (one-time only, $200 rescheduling fee applies)

Refund Process

  • Refunds are processed within 14 business days of cancellation
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Processing fees are non-refundable
  • Bank processing times may vary (typically 5-10 business days)

Exceptions & Special Circumstances

Weather Emergencies

In case of severe weather warnings or natural disasters that prevent safe travel, we will work with you to reschedule or provide a full refund. Documentation from official sources may be required.

Medical Emergencies

Medical emergencies affecting the primary guest may qualify for special consideration. Medical documentation from a licensed physician will be required. Refund amount determined on case-by-case basis.

Property Issues

If Mountain View Mansion becomes unavailable due to property damage, emergency repairs, or other unforeseen circumstances, we will provide a full refund or offer comparable alternative accommodations.

Modification Policy

Date changes are subject to availability and may incur additional charges if the new dates have higher rates. Contact us at least 60 days before check-in to request modifications.

  • Date changes: $200 modification fee + any rate difference
  • Guest count changes: Must be approved and may affect pricing
  • Event type changes: Subject to approval and terms

No-Show Policy

Guests who do not check in and do not cancel before check-in time will be charged the full amount of the reservation with no refund.

Travel Insurance Recommendation

We Strongly Recommend Travel Insurance

Travel insurance can protect your investment in case of unexpected illness, family emergencies, travel delays, or other unforeseen circumstances. Most policies cost 5-7% of your trip total and provide comprehensive coverage including cancellation protection.

How to Cancel

To cancel your reservation, please contact us as soon as possible:

  • Phone: +1 (425) 941-6203
  • Email: stay@mvmansion.com
  • Cancellations must be received in writing (email confirmation required)
